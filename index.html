Made for artists, writers and thinkers.

Suda is an independent specialty coffee shop in Camden, one of the most vibrant places in London.

Suda means 'chit chat' in Korean, so we’d like to provide our customers with a joyful place where they can chat and have delicious food, drinks and dessert.

About Us

We collaborate with local independent makers to create quality green items. Everything you buy from us at Suda Bakery is both ethically-made and environmentally friendly, and will go towards making the world a better place. It all started with a cup of coffee, a song, and an idea.
